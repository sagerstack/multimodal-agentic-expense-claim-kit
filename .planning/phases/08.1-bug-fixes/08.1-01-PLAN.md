<objective>
Restructure the claim status lifecycle from flat statuses (approved/rejected/escalated) to the 8-state model: draft -> pending -> ai_reviewed -> ai_approved/ai_rejected/escalated -> manually_approved/manually_rejected. This is a foundational change that all other plans in this phase depend on.

Purpose: The current flat statuses conflate AI decisions with human decisions. The 8-state lifecycle makes it clear who made each decision and at what stage the claim is in the pipeline.
Output: All status references across advisor agent, review router, dashboard router, and templates updated to the new 8-state model.
</objective>
